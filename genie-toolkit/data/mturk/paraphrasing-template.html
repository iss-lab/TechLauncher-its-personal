Instructions

We are building a virtual assistant that can help you control all your devices, social accounts, etc. Our virtual assistant can do a lot of things now but it still has a hard time to understand the users since different users may say the same thing very differently. So we need you to help us teach the assistant.

In the following, you will see 4 different sentences, and you will be asked to paraphrase each of them in 2 different ways without changing the meaning of the sentence, which means

  • Do not remove the device/service names if they exist in the original sentence
  • Do not change the meaning of the sentence even if doesn't seem to be useful
  • Do not drop any information unless it's obvious without context;
  • Do not add any new information that doesn't exist in the sentence;
  • Keep quoted parts (including the quote marks), emails, phone numbers identical
  • Do not drop @ signs and # signs
Warning: for the last two requirements, we have a script to verify and if you failed to meet them, the script will reject your answer

Note that since the sentences are generated by a computer, it sometimes may be unnatural and clunky. Try to make the paraphrase natural, for example:
  • Drop unnecessary components, e.g., "send an email with some subject" -> "send an email" ("with some subject" does not give the virtual assistant any additional information)
  • Fix grammar mistakes

Try your best to make the 2 paraphrases as different as possible. If you really don't know how to paraphrase, leave "no idea". According to our stats, we only got 5% of "no idea"s. So if you give us too many of them, we might reject you.

Example 

send a direct message to @bob saying "hello" on twitter
Correct paraphrase 1: 
direct message @bob on twitter with the message "hello"
Correct paraphrase 2: 
say "hello" to @bob via direct message on twitter
Wrong paraphrase 1: (missing service name "twitter") 
say "hello" to @bob
Wrong paraphrase 2: (missing quotes for "hello" and the @ sign for "@bob") 
say hello to bob on twitter

Your turn now
