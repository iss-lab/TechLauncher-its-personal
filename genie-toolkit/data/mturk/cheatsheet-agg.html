Instructions

We are building a voice personal virtual assistant that can help you control all your devices, social accounts, etc.

In the following, you will see a cheatsheet containing some of the devices and functions our virtual assistant supports. We need you to write us some examples that computing the results returned by theses functions. The supported computation includes

  • maximum
  • minimum
  • average
  • sum
  • count

Examples:

Example 1 (maximum):
  • Show me my Dropbox file that changed most recently.
Example 2 (average):
  • Notify me when my average file size in Dropbox becomes smaller than 10KB.
Example 3 (count):
  • How many files do i have in my Dropbox?

Your turn now

Note that once you click the "START" button, the cheatsheet will be removed. You will not be able to read them again.
