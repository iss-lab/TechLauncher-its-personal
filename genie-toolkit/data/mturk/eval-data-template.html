Instructions

We have a database containing information about hotels (like those on the Booking.com website), we would like you to help us come up with questions to query against it.

The database contains the following properties for each hotel:

  • Name: the name of the hotel.
  • Brand: the brand of the hotel (e.g., Hyatt Place).
  • Description: the description of the hotel.
  • Telephone: the telephone number of the hotel.
  • Rating: the overall rating of the hotel, in range 0-5.
  • Review_number: the number of reviews posted about the hotel.
  • Check_in_time: the earliest checkin time of the hotel (e.g., 03:00 PM).
  • Check_out_time: the latest checkout time of the hotel (e.g., 11:00 AM).
  • Location: the address and location of the hotel (including postal code, street name and number, city, state, conuntry).
  • Services: the characteristics or services provided by the hotel (e.g., Free Internet Access, Free Parking, Free Breakfast, Fitness Center).

Please come up with 5 examples of questions to search/find/query hotels from our database according to certain properties. In each of your question, please include
exactly two properties. Besides, the last one of your questions should be a ranking question (see Example 5).

Examples:

Example 1:
  • What is the rating of Hotel Zephyr San Francisco?
    • (two properties: Name, Rating)
Example 2:
  • Search for Hyatt hotels with at least 100 reviews.
    • (two properties: Brand, Review_number)
Example 3:
  • Search for 5-star hotels nearby.
    • (two properties: Rating, Address)
Example 4:
  • Show me all the hotels which provide free breakfast and the checkin time is earlier than 3 PM.
    • (two properties: Services, Check_in_time)
Example 5 (ranking):
  • Show me the description of the nearest hotel?
    • (two properties: Description, Address)

Your turn now

When writing your questions, remeber to include at least 2 properties as in the above examples. If more than 2 questions you wrote fail to meet the criteria, your answer might be rejected.

You can answer this task for multiple times. However, multiple submissions must have all different answers, otherwise your submissions will be rejected.
