Instructions

We are building a voice personal virtual assistant that can help you control all your devices, social accounts, etc.

In the following, you will see a cheatsheet containing one of the devices our virtual assistant supports - Spotify. It has three different types of functions:

  • when command: which decides when the rule will trigger, e.g., when it rains, when new article posts.
  • get command: get something from the device/service, e.g., get my latest email, get the current weather.
  • do command: perform an action on the device/service, e.g., send an email, turn off my camera.;

In the following, we will ask you to give us 5 example commands to demonstrate how you will use this.

You can simply adopt any of the commands in the cheatsheet, and replace the placeholder with things you like, such as your favourite songs or artists. You can also combine two commands together to create a rule to simplify your life. Here are some examples.

Examples:

Example 1:
  • Play shake it off
Example 2:
  • What is the tempo of Hey Jude?
Example 3 (a rule combines two function):
  • when: when a song is playing
  • do: add ____ (song name) to my playlist ___ (playlist) ;
  • Command: Add all played song to my playlist my history
Bad example (do not leave blanks, replace it with something you like):
  • Play the artist _____

Your turn now

Now take your time to read through the cheatsheet. Click "START" button if you are ready, and then you will be asked to provide 5 examples that using one or two functions in the cheatsheet.

Note that once you click the "START" button, the cheatsheet will be removed. You will not be able to read them again.

When writing your commands, do not keep the "when", "get", and "do" in the cheatsheet in front of each example; do not leave any blanks in your commands, fill them with something you think it's reasonable; do not use functions that are not in the cheatsheet. If more than 2 commands you wrote fail to meet the criteria, your answer might be rejected.

You can only do this task once. Multiple submissions from the same user ID will be rejected.

No need to write sentences that are exactly the same in the cheatsheet. We want you to know how would you say it in your way in real life. In fact, we would love to have more variety on how different people express the same idea. So be creative.
